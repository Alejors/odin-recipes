Guacamole

Description

Mexican dipping sauces are well-known for being delicious. They are suitable for almost any meeting. Watching a football game, game nights or inviting in-laws over. Now well make Guacamole, the most famous avocado dipping sauce of all times.

Ingredients

  • 2 Avocados (ripe)
  • 1/4 onion (chopped)
  • Coriander (chopped)
  • 1 lemon (squeezed)
  • 1/2 tomato (thinly chopped)
  • salt
  • optional: jalapeno (chopped)

Steps

  1. Peel and remove the seeds from the avocados.
  2. Crush them in a bowl until you get the desired texture.
  3. Add the onion, coriander, tomatos and jalapenoes.
  4. Season with the juice from the lemon and the salt.
  5. Dip some tortillas or nachos with the Guacamole.
